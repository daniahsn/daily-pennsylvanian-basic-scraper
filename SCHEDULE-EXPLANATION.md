## Understanding the Cron Expression

The cron expression `"0 20 * * *"` means:

- **0** → The job starts at **minute 0** of the hour.
- **20** → The job runs at **20:00 UTC (8:00 PM UTC)**.
- **\*** → Runs on **any day of the month**.
- **\*** → Runs in **any month**.
- **\*** → Runs on **any day of the week**.
